A clinical, high-contrast left-hand sidebar for a medical iPad application. It contains a searchable inventory of medical devices categorized by type (Intravascular, Structural, etc.). The design must be extremely clean, using a white/light-gray background with dark slate text for maximum legibility in an operating room environment. 

**Page Structure:**
1. Search bar at the top.
2. Accordion/collapsible categories.
3. Grid of simplified abstract medical device icons.
